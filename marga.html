engine MARGA — one signage system, three movements: directional signs walk (one unbroken meander), place signs grow (a single line, grown by accumulation), notices settle (strata of an imagined terrain).
every sign is seeded by its own words — the catalogue is the grammar. hierarchy is etch time: the entrance is grown for hours, a bay label in minutes.
linework is vector-etched, lettering engraved — wood, glass or stone; any typeface.
