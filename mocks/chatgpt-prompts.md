# LIPI mockup prompts for ChatGPT

Attachments per prompt:
- Prompt 1 → `lipi_longwall_seed7.png`
- Prompt 2 → `lipi_panelseries_seed7.png` (all seven panels in a row, text flowing across them)
- Prompt 3 → `lipi_longwall_seed7.png` + `lipi_glossary_seed7.png`
- Prompt 4 → `lipi_glossary_seed7.png` (+ longwall for the background)

Ask for landscape / wide aspect unless noted.

---

## 1 · The curved wall (hero shot)

> Photorealistic architectural interior photograph of a large modern university
> library lobby in India. A long, gently curved feature wall (about 12 metres)
> sweeps through the space, finished in warm off-white lime plaster. The entire
> wall is covered edge to edge in horizontal ruled lines of a mysterious
> minimal script, hand-drawn in fine black ink — use the attached image as the
> exact artwork on the wall: preserve its letterforms, spacing and line
> structure precisely, do not invent different glyphs. The script wraps
> smoothly around the wall's curve. Double-height ceiling with warm wood
> slats, polished grey concrete floor, tall bookshelves visible in the
> background, soft diffused daylight from a clerestory. Two or three students
> in the middle distance, one standing close to the wall reading it
> attentively. Calm, contemplative, slightly reverent atmosphere. Shot on a
> 35mm lens, eye level, wide landscape composition.

## 2 · The panel series

> Photorealistic interior photograph of a contemporary Indian university
> library lobby. Along one long straight wall hangs a series of seven large
> framed panels (A1 size, portrait, thin natural-wood frames, generous white
> mats), evenly spaced at reading height with small brass picture lights
> above each. The attached image shows all seven panels in order — use it as
> the exact artwork: panel one on the left through panel seven on the right,
> preserving each panel's letterforms and layout precisely, do not invent
> different glyphs. The text flows from one panel into the next, like pages
> of a single letter. Warm white wall, terrazzo floor, a long wooden reading bench
> facing the panels with one seated reader looking up at them. Soft morning
> light raking along the wall. Architectural photography, wide landscape.

## 3 · The decoder (detail shot)

> Photorealistic close-up photograph inside a university library lobby. In
> sharp focus: a student's hand holding a small brass-framed key card — use
> the second attached image (a chart pairing unknown glyphs with the letters
> A–Z and 0–9) as exactly what is printed on the card. Behind and slightly
> out of focus: a plastered wall covered in ruled lines of the same unknown
> script drawn in black ink — use the first attached image as that wall
> artwork. The student's other hand touches the wall, finger under one glyph,
> mid-decode. Warm daylight, shallow depth of field, 50mm lens. Quiet,
> intimate, the feeling of solving something slowly.

## 4 · The glossary plate on its stand

> Photorealistic photograph of a small elegant museum-style interpretation
> stand in a library lobby: a brushed brass plate (about A4) mounted at a
> 30-degree angle on a slim dark-steel lectern stand. Engraved on the plate:
> use the attached chart image exactly — a grid of unknown glyphs, each with
> its plain letter beneath, titled "LIPI 7 · GLOSSARY". In the background,
> softly blurred, a long plastered wall covered in lines of the same script.
> Warm ambient light, shallow depth of field, product-photography quality.
