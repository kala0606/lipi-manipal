## 1 · The entrance monolith (hero shot)

> Photorealistic architectural photograph of the entrance of a large modern
> university library in India, early morning. Beside the main glass doors
> stands a tall freestanding monolith sign, about 600 mm wide and 1.5 m tall,
> made of thick birch plywood with a fine dark laser-etched artwork — use the
> attached image as exactly what is etched on its face: the words "MANIPAL
> LIBRARY" at the top and a dense organic single-line pattern below, preserve
> the pattern and lettering precisely, do not redraw or reinterpret. The
> etched line is slightly burnt-brown, matte, tactile. Warm brick and concrete
> architecture, students walking in with bags, soft golden light raking across
> the plywood showing the relief of the etching. Shot on a 35 mm lens, eye
> level, portrait or wide as suits.
